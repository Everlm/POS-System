
## Overview

This project focuses on developing the backend of a point of sale system, with the aim of increasing business productivity by allowing for quick and efficient billing, and keeping a complete record of revenue generated at the point of sale. Additionally, the solution seeks to offer precise control of the company's product inventory. With this solution, we hope to improve business efficiency and profitability by optimizing sales and inventory management processes
